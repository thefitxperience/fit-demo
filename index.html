DAILY NUTRITION & LIFESTYLE GUIDELINES

These guidelines are designed to support your meal plan by focusing on daily habits that improve energy, digestion, recovery, and long-term health.

Daily Foundations Summary

Proper hydration supports digestion, appetite regulation, energy levels, skin health, and mental focus.

  1. Upon waking: 1 glass of water.
  2. Morning: 1–2 glasses between breakfast and lunch.
  3. Afternoon: 1–2 glasses between lunch and dinner.
  4. Evening: 1 glass, stopping at least 1 hour before bed.
  5. Daily target: Women 1.8–2.2 L / Men 2.2–2.8 L.

Hydration

Proper hydration supports digestion, appetite regulation, energy levels, skin health, and mental focus. Upon waking, drink one glass of water. In the morning, drink one to two glasses between breakfast and lunch. In the afternoon, drink one to two glasses between lunch and dinner. In the evening, drink one glass, stopping at least one hour before bed. The daily target is approximately 1.8 to 2.2 liters for women and 2.2 to 2.8 liters for men.

Protein Intake

Protein supports muscle preservation, metabolism, blood sugar balance, and satiety. Aim for approximately 1.2 to 1.6 grams of protein per kilogram of body weight per day. Include a clear protein source at every main meal.

Animal-Based Proteins

  • Eggs / Egg Whites
  • Chicken / Turkey
  • Beef / Steak
  • Salmon / Tuna
  • White Fish
  • Shrimp / Seafood
  • Greek Yogurt / Labneh
  • Cottage Cheese

Plant-Based Proteins

  • Lentils
  • Chickpeas
  • Beans
  • Tofu / Tempeh
  • Quinoa
  • Edamame
  • Nuts & Seeds

Vegetables & Fruits

Vegetables provide fiber, vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants essential for digestion and overall health. Aim for 6–8 servings daily. Fruits provide natural sugars and antioxidants; aim for 2–3 servings daily.

Vegetables

  • Spinach, arugula, kale
  • Lettuce varieties
  • Broccoli, cauliflower
  • Zucchini, eggplant
  • Carrots, beets
  • Mushrooms
  • Green beans
  • Bell peppers
  • Tomatoes, cucumbers
  • Onions, garlic

Fruits

  • Berries
  • Apples, pears
  • Oranges, mandarins
  • Banana
  • Mango, pineapple
  • Grapes, cherries
  • Melon

DIET PLAN
DAILY NUTRITION & LIFESTYLE GUIDELINES

Complex Carbohydrates

Complex carbohydrates fuel the brain, support hormonal health, and aid recovery. Best consumed in the morning and post-activity.

  • Rice (white, brown, black)
  • Potatoes & sweet potatoes
  • Quinoa
  • Bulgur
  • Oats
  • Pasta (moderate portions)
  • Whole-grain bread
  • Beans & lentils

Healthy Fats

Healthy fats support hormone production, nutrient absorption, brain function, and satiety. Include 1–2 sources daily.

  • Olive oil
  • Avocado
  • Nuts & seeds
  • Nut butters
  • Fatty fish
  • Cheese (non-processed)

Sleep & Recovery

Aim for 7–9 hours of sleep per night. Consistent sleep and wake times support appetite regulation, recovery, and overall well-being.
